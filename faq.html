Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your journey with TradeEdge.

Is this course suitable for complete beginners?
Yes. While we move into advanced institutional concepts quickly, Module 1 is designed to build a solid foundation for anyone who has never placed a trade before.
Do I need a large capital to start trading?
Not at all. In the course, we specifically focus on Proprietary Trading Firms, where you can manage accounts worth $50,000 or more for a very small evaluation fee.
What markets do we focus on?
The primary focus is on the Nasdaq (NAS100) due to its high volatility and clean technical setups during the New York session.
Is there a community or mentorship included?
Yes! Every student gets lifetime access to our private Discord community where I share daily market updates and answer questions.
